How to Play

You will be presented with a list of challengers. Select one, review their stats, and initiate the match!

You’ll have 60 seconds to answer all five questions. Watch the timer at the top of your screen—if you answer the most questions correctly in the shortest time, you’ll win! Play every day to improve your rating and earn Badges. Badges have multiple levels available for even more chances to earn.

You can play five matches each day with multiple opportunities to earn more! Remember to visit and duel often. If you’re inactive for too long, your rating will go down.
